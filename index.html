Welcome to the Moroccan Mathematicians Network

A platform dedicated to promoting mathematical research in Morocco

Our Mission

The Moroccan Mathematicians Network (MMN) is a dynamic community bringing together researchers, professors, students, and professionals in the field of mathematics in Morocco and internationally.

Our mission is to promote mathematical research, facilitate collaborations, and enhance Moroccan scientific excellence in this field.
